Getting Started

Playing a station is simple: just click any station name in the list on the left. It starts playing immediately.

Use the Stop button or press Space to stop playback. Click another station to switch.

The volume slider on the right adjusts loudness.

Tip: Enable "Balloon Help" from the Help menu to see explanations when you hover over any control.

Managing Stations

Click Add Station... or press ⌘N to add a new station. You need a name and a direct stream URL (usually ending in /stream or similar).

To remove a station, select it and click the Remove button.

Your stations are saved in your browser and will be remembered next time you visit.

The Visualizer

The spectrum analyzer responds to the music with animated bars or waveforms. Choose from four styles in the View menu:

Rainbow Spectrum -- each frequency band gets its own color, from red (bass) to purple (treble).

VU Meter -- classic Winamp-style green-yellow-red gradient based on amplitude.

CRT Phosphor -- green-on-black terminal aesthetic with glow effects.

Oscilloscope -- real-time waveform display.

Radio Garden Help

Getting Started

Click on a station in the list to begin playing. Use the transport controls to play and stop.

Adding Stations

Click "Add Station..." or press ⌘N to add a new station. You need the station name and a direct stream URL (usually ending in /stream or similar).

Keyboard Shortcuts

⌘N — New Station
Space — Play/Stop
⌘V — Toggle Visualizer
